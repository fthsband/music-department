ATTENDANCE

If you are unable to attend a music department event, such as a rehearsal, football game, parade, or concert, please fill out this form. This form is due 48 hours before the event.

Attendance at ALL performances and rehearsals are mandatory.


Attendance at all rehearsals and performances is mandatory for credit. An absence will only be excused for the following reasons: illness, injury, death in family, or a school-related conflict. Students may not accrue more than 3 excused absences / marking period.

If the aforementioned absence form is not submitted within this time, the student will be charged with an “unexcused absence.” Any absence not brought to the attention of the director for approval prior to the commitment date will be considered “unexcused”. An unexcused absence carries a penalty of a zero (0) for that day's activity (rehearsal or performance) and an additional ten (10) points off the student's final marking period grade. Only in cases of extreme emergency or extreme illness will a student be excused from this penalty

Students must attend any and all dress rehearsals. If the student fails to attend the dress rehearsal, he/she will receive a failing grade and may not perform.

For students participating in an extracurricular fashion, three (3) unexcused absences will result in dismissal from the band.

Unapproved lateness to band functions will not be tolerated. Any student who desires to be excused for part of a rehearsal must follow the same procedure as above and fill out the appropriate excuse form. Any student who fails to receive approval for a tardy will have points deducted from that day's activity grade. Upon the third late, the student will be charged with an unexcused absence.

Being in the band means that you have agreed to be here on many special days, and the state bowl game. All upperclassmen are reminded to make P-SAT and SAT plans around the band calendar.

Regular attendance in this class is necessary and required to receive credit in the course. Course credit will be withdrawn if a student's total number of absences (excused and unexcused) is excessive: 20 for a full year's course. Course credit will also be withdrawn if the total number of absences due to cuts or truancy exceeds 3 in a full year course. Parents will be notified of student absences, per FRHSD Policy, throughout the school year.
