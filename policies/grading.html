GRADING POLICY

Grading Breakdown

Tests and Assessments - 40%

Classwork and Prep - 30%

Homework and Attendance - 30%

Emphasis is on the following areas:

-- Posture (sitting)

Both feet flat on floor
Hand position
Sitting on edge of chair
Back straight

-- Posture (standing)

Head up
Shoulders straight
Legs straight
Knees unlocked
Horn angle

-- Phrasing

Sustains entire phrase
Releases ends of phrases cleanly
Maintains balanced dynamic levels

-- Rhythmic Accuracy

Maintains steady tempo
Sustains full value of notes
Performs proper note values
Performs in proper style / feel

-- Intonation

Maintains intonation in all tessituras
High, middle, and low register
Clear tone

-- Dress Code and Attendance

Follows dress code for both marching and concert band
Attends all rehearsals and performances

-- Articulation

Articulation of beginning of phrase
Proper articulation of legato passages
Proper articulation of tenuto passages
Proper articulation of staccato
Proper articulation of accents
Proper articulation of slurs
Proper articulation of marcato

-- Rehearsal Etiquette

Does not distract others
Attempts corrections as directed
Begins and ends with conductor
Attentive throughout rehearsal
Maintains place in music
Does not wear hats
No cellphone usage

-- Ensemble Technique

Performs within the ensemble balance
Performs within the ensemble interpretation
Responds to conductor's gesture

-- Note Accuracy

Knows fingerings / positions / stickings
Plays in proper register
Accurate in high, middle, and low register
Remains within established key
Responds accurately to accidentals

-- Marching Band

Music memorization
Properly prepared drill book
