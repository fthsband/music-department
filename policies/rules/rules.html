RULES FOR BAND MEMBERS

Join the Remind and read the posting board outside of the band room door. The Remind code can be found on this website's home page.

Show goodwill towards fellow bandsmen and other musician-peers at all times.

Take performances and rehearsal seriously. Remain in uniform.

Come to rehearsal prepared. Know your routines, music, drill, etc.

No talking or disturbances of any kind will be tolerated during rehearsals.

Keep the band room, music suite and school clean and orderly.

Keep music in order where it can be reached with little confusion for rehearsals and performances. Do not leave music on the stands, instruments unattended, or where they don't belong.

Have a pencil and check for special equipment needed before rehearsal begins (valve oil, lyre, flip-folder, reeds, drum sticks, etc).

Do not attract attention to yourself when representing the band. Such as loud playing/blasting of instruments, screaming, etc.

The drum line shall maintain a reasonable volume indoors.

If you have a documented health condition that requires medication, specifically epi-pen devices, inhalers, diabetic equipment, this condition must be on file with doctor's order etc with our health office before that student may travel or perform with the FTHS Band.
