PEP MUSIC

PEP Music is an artistic “special” the students of our Patriot Employment Program can explore while at FTHS. The course is rooted in music education with highlights in music therapy.

Our goal is to provide to our students positive, meaningful learning experiences through music: singing, dancing, listening, and creating.

We begin class each day with stretches and counting in sequence.

We almost always have some amount of silent or “quiet time” early in our class.

Our class thereafter consists mostly of DANCING and lots of movement. This is the single most important aspect of our class.

Each dancing activity is interspersed with lessons on speech, singing, musical concepts, music instruments, and learning how to listen.

Students are assessed on

Class participation
Demonstrating improvement in any number of explored areas (coordination, singing, etc).
Following directions
