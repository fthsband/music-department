Freehold Township Music Department

MUSIC PARENTS ASSOCIATION

The Freehold Township Music Parents Organization supports the music department students, programs and teachers. Its primary goal is to promote pride and interest in, and support the musical organizations of FTHS. Music Parents act as chaperones for the football games, parades and festivals. Music Parents also fundraise to provide financial assistance when needed for such items as equipment, scholarships for higher education and logistics such as drinks and snacks for students at music events.


Any parent interested in becoming an active member of an organization that actively supports the arts and musicians of our school, contact Ms. Janet Farrell, President, at fthsmusicparents@gmail.com.
